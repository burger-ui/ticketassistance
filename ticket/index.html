How It Works
Process & Information

Simple, transparent, and stress-free from inquiry to entry.

Step-by-Step
Our Process
1
Step 1
Inquiry
Send us your event details — artist, date, venue, preferred section, and number of tickets. We'll confirm availability and send you a quote within 2 hours.
2
Step 2
Confirmation
Once you agree to the quote and service fee, we confirm your slot. You'll receive a booking reference and timeline for your specific event.
3
Step 3
Payment
Complete your payment via your preferred method. We accept GCash, PayPal, bank transfer, and more. Receipts are sent immediately. Ticket payment and service fee are handled separately.
4
Step 4
Assistance Starts
Our team queues for your tickets during presale or general admission. We send you live updates and screenshots throughout the process so you're never left guessing.
5
Step 5
Completion
Your QR-code ticket is delivered to your email and app. We send a final proof-of-purchase screenshot and your booking summary. You're ready for the show!
🔒
Privacy & Confidentiality Your personal information — name, contact details, and payment records — is never shared with third parties. All data is handled in compliance with data privacy regulations. Communications are kept strictly between you and our team. We do not store card numbers; all payments go through secure, encrypted gateways.
